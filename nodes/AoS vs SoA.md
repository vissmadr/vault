# AoS vs SoA

(Arrays of Structures vs Structures of Arrays)

The choice between AoS versus SoA.

---

## Arrays of Structures

The [[Object Oriented Design]].

Kinda more intuitive, especially for beginners.

Worse performance.

## Structures of Arrays

The [[Data Oriented Design]].

Great performance due to data oriented principles.

Promotes modularity and efficient data structures.
