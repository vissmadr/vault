# Comparative Zero-sum Difficulty

Competitive rulesets and zero-sum games cannot be "easier" or "more difficult" by definition.

- If the game is easy for you, it is also easy for your opponent.
- If the game is difficult for you, it is also difficult for your opponent.

---

The true objective, as an individual, is to win against your competition.

Therefore the **shared difficulty** of the ruleset does not matter.
