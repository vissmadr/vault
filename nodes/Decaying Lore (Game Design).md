# Decaying Lore (Game Design)

Storytelling design pattern that I noticed in both [[The Talos Principle]] and [[Duskers]].

---

Both games have pieces of lore that are eroding due to some form of decay in the story.

In The Talos Principle, the world itself is a computer system that is getting corrupted over time.
Something similar seems to be happening in Duskers, where the lore that you find is also corrupted.

The developers of both games made the corruption very well, meaning that you can feel the gaps of missing information, but you can also somewhat understand what the overall context is about.

This really adds to the immersion and exploration of both games.
