# Digital Technology

Technologies that operate using discrete values, typically [[Binary]] data.

---

Relies on electronic systems that process, store, and transmit information in digital form, most commonly through a [[Computer]] and a [[Computer Network]].

**Ecosystem**: Digital technology is an ecosystem of [[Hardware]] and [[Software]] working together.
