# Large Language Model (LLM)

Large-scale AI model trained to understand and generate human language.

---

High-capacity [[Neural Network]] trained on vast amounts of text to internalize linguistic patterns, world knowledge, and semantic relationships.

**Limitations**: Hallucinations, sensitivity to input phrasing, and imperfect reasoning.
