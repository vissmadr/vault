# Ravenswatch

Action roguelike with strong combat feel, distinct hero kits, and fairytale-inspired character design.

---

**Heroes**: Character kits feel meaningfully different, not just cosmetically different.
. The playstyle of each hero is drastically different than the others.
. Even the heroes have multiple different playstyles each.

**Combat**: Heavy, slow, and readable. Abilities have weight and good audiovisual feedback.

**Theme**: Uses twisted fairy tale / folklore characters in a way that gives the game strong identity.

**Greed Design**: One of the core designs in the game is balancing between greed and survival, between long-term goals and short-term necessities.
