# Wrestling Stance

Legs are in natural width.
One leg slightly forward.
Bend the knees.
Weight is balanced on both legs.
Weight is in the middle.
Body is forward, but back is straight.

Should be stable and ready.

When moving, keep the same leg configuration.
Do not cross the legs.
Do not go out of position.

The lead leg needs to penetrate.
The back leg needs to drive.
